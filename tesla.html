Tesla Motors

Web & Mobile




During summer 2016, I was an UI/UX design intern at Tesla where I worked on three projects. The first project I worked was redesigning the Device Transmittal Manager, a database that helps Tesla engineering define and communicate electrical interface information and is being first introduced for Model 3 development. The second project I worked on was a feature for Tesla's mobile app. The last project I worked on was redesigning and redefining Tesla Toolbox, an enterprise tool to help troubleshoot.

I learned a tremendous amount from working on an internal tool, consumer product, and enterprise tool. Special thanks to all my amazing managers and mentors for fostering my growth during my time at Tesla.
